About Us

This is the founding chapter of ColorStack at Purdue University Indianapolis, a student-led community dedicated to empowering Black, Latinx, and Indigenous students in tech.

Our mission is to create a supportive space where students of color can thrive academically, professionally, and personally.

Through mentorship, career development, networking events, and collaborative projects, we aim to close the equity gap in computing and technology fields.
By working together and lifting each other up, we’re building a future where diversity isn’t just celebrated—it’s the standard.

Join us as we grow a vibrant, inclusive tech community right here on campus.
